Why Japan?

Let the rising sun bloom

Japan is one of the oldest civilizations and has a beautiful and diverse history. The stunning, diverse scenery with mountains and breathtaking views, which are much appreciated by the Japanese, offers so many different experiences that attract tourists from all corners of the world.

There are around 100,000 Shinto shrines and 80,000 Buddhist temples throughout Japan where locals visit and pray. They are some of the most peaceful locations you're likely to find. The picture on the left shows one of the many torii gates at the famous Fushimi Inari shrine located in the city of Kyoto.
